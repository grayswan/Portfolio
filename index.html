What I do

I'm experienced with HTML5, CSS3, Sass, Javascript, jQuery, Backbone, and React. I've utilized various build tools and pre-processors, and am very familiar with GitHub. I won't stop until I've found the perfect color/font/image combination. But most importantly, I'm always learning.

Who I am

After several years of working in compliance at a social service agency, I decided to switch careers to explore my creative side by delving into the fabulous world of development via The Iron Yard. I'm forever interested in people and the intersting turns life can take, and believe that clear communication is paramount to any and all relationships, business or personal. Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my husband and delighting in the hilarious and wild antics of our three little boys, dreaming of awesome places to travel, spending time in nature, engaging in spontaneous dance parties, dabbling in interior design, tending to my large collection of plants, and always aiming to be perpetually joyful and perfectly present.
